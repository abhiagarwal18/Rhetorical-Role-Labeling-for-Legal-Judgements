d10_s8	An FIR was registered at 11:45 am on 11.01.2010 on the basis of statement given by Sadiq Hussain Majloom Jafri (PW-3).

d10_s37	Sprouting of blood seen on left side of chest, shoulder and back. 
d10_s38	Carotid vessel torn and retracted on left side."

d10_s40	Charge-sheet was filed against all the four accused and thereafter charges were framed for an offence punishable under Section 302 read with Section 34 IPC. 
d10_s41	The Trial Court convicted the Appellant in Criminal Appeal No. 430 of 2014 for an offence punishable under Section 302 and sentenced him to life imprisonment. 
d10_s42	The other accused who are Appellants in Criminal Appeal Nos. 458-459 of 2014 were acquitted by the Trial Court. 
d10_s43	As stated earlier, the conviction and sentence of the Appellant in Criminal Appeal No. 430 of 2014 was confirmed by the High Court. 
d10_s44	The acquittal of the Appellants in Criminal Appeal Nos.

d10_s46	The deceased Sarfraj was having a shop at Jamkhed where he was selling goggles. 
d10_s47	He was the maternal uncle of PW-3 who is also in the same business. 
d10_s48	PW-3 was being helped by the deceased for selling goggles in the Ahmednagar area. 
d10_s49	The case of the prosecution is that there was rivalry between the accused and the deceased as they were in the same business.

d10_s53	Sikardar Ali (A-1) and Shahjahan Ali (A-2) were caught and handed over to the police at the spot.

d10_s67	We have no doubt about the complicity of all the accused in the homicide of Sarfraj. 
d10_s68	A-1 attacked the deceased with the knife and caused injury on his neck which resulted in his death. 
d10_s69	The other accused assisted him in committing the crime by holding the hands of the deceased. 
d10_s70	However, the only question that falls for our consideration is whether the accused are liable to be punished for an offence under Section 302 IPC. 
d10_s71	After considering the submissions made by the counsel for the Appellants and scrutinising the material on record, we are of the opinion that the accused are not liable to be convicted under Section 302 IPC. 
d10_s72	We are convinced that there was neither prior concert nor common intention to commit a murder. 
d10_s73	During the course of their business activity the accused reached the dhaba where the deceased was present. 
d10_s74	An altercation took place during the discussion they were having behind the dhaba.

d10_s76	Exception 4 to Section 300 is applicable to the facts of this case. 
d10_s77	As we are convinced that the accused are responsible for the death of Sarfraj, we are of the opinion that they are liable for conviction under Section 304 part II of the IPC. 
d10_s78	We are informed that A-1 has undergone a sentence of seven years and that A-2 to A-4 have undergone four years of imprisonment. 
d10_s79	We modify the judgment of the High Court converting the conviction of the accused from Section 302 to Section 304 part II of the IPC sentencing them to the period already undergone. 
d10_s80	They shall be released forthwith.
